Our Story

A Kitchen Built on Family Tradition

Mugdha Caterers began in Chinchwad with a simple promise — every dish leaving our kitchen would be as wholesome as a meal cooked at home, and as generous as one served at a Maharashtrian wedding.

Today, led by Mukund and a dedicated team of chefs, we cater weddings, munj, baby showers, birthdays and corporate events across Moshi, Chinchwad and greater Pune, without ever compromising on our pure vegetarian promise.
