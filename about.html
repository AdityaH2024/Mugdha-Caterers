Company Story

From a Home Kitchen to Pune's Trusted Caterer

Mugdha Caterers was founded in Chinchwad with one belief: that a caterer's job is not just to feed guests, but to make a family's celebration feel effortless. What started as a small operation cooking for neighbourhood functions has grown into a full-service catering team trusted across Moshi, Chinchwad and PCMC.

Every recipe on our menu has been tested, tasted and refined by our own kitchen — from the welcome sharbat that greets your guests to the last spoon of shrikhand served at the end of the meal.

We cater weddings, munj, vastu shanti, birthday parties, baby showers and every occasion in between — always 100% pure vegetarian, always made fresh.
